About TrackTime

Why I made this

I like F1. Every time I want to find the race time, I end up doing all sorts of mental math comparing timezones to make sure I get it right. I'm hoping this website can be used to avoid additional headaches by providing all race, practice, and qualifying times in your local timezone.

I created this as a learning experience using Python/Django for the first time.
